Selecting appropriate words

Fill in the blanks by selecting the right word

 
  1. Seema _______ know how to swim.
    1.   doesn't
    2.   don't
  2. He is _______ on the sofa.
    1.   lying
    2.   laying
  3. I _______ my best to help him.
    1.   done
    2.   did
  4. They _______ that Jimmy is not home.
    1.   told
    2.   said
  5. Adrian _______ speak English well.
    1.   doesn't
    2.   don't
  6. Fuel prices are on the _______.
    1.   rise
    2.   raise
  7. I _______ a mistake.
    1.   made
    2.   did
  8. She always _______ the truth.
    1.   says
    2.   tells
  9. _______ I come with you?
    1.   Shall
    2.   Will
  10. He has _______ all the arrangements.
    1.   done
    2.   made
