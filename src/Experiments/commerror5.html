Confused words
Instructions:

For each question, select the correct sentence from the two given.
When the quiz is graded, the correct answers will appear after each question.

1. Remain and stay B
a. We remained in a big hotel.
b. We stayed in a big hotel.

2. Put on and wear B
a. Tina always puts on brown shoes.
b. Tina always wears brown shoes.

3. Pick and pick up A
a. They picked cherries in the garden.
b. They picked up cherries in the garden.

4. Borrow and lend B
a. Could you please borrow me some money?
b. Could you please lend me some money?

5. Steal and rob B
a. Some men stole a bank the other night.
b. Some men robbed a bank the other night.

6. Refuse and deny A
a. Simanta refused to take the reward.
b. Simanta denied to take the reward.

7. Persuade and convince B
a. I am not persuaded of John's innocence.
b. I am not convinced of John's innocence.

8. Deals in and deals with A
a. A jeweller deals in jewellery.
b. A jeweller deals with jewellery.

9. Sit and seat A
a. We sit on a chair.
b. We seat on a chair.

10. Make and do B
a. Can you make your work?
b. Can you do your work?
